About the Members

Steve Rogers, also known as Captain America, is the leader of the Avengers. He is a super-soldier with enhanced strength, agility, and endurance.

Tony Stark, known as Iron Man, is a genius engineer and billionaire. He uses his intellect and resources to create advanced suits of armor.

Thor Odinson is the Norse god of thunder. He wields the powerful hammer Mjolnir and is known for his strength and combat skills.

Bruce Banner, also known as the Hulk, is a brilliant scientist who transforms into a powerful green giant when angered.

Natasha Romanoff, or Black Widow, is a highly skilled spy and martial artist. She has a mysterious past and is an expert in espionage.

Clint Barton, known as Hawkeye, is a master archer with exceptional marksmanship skills. He is a former S.H.I.E.L.D. agent and a valuable member of the team.
